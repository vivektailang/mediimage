Our Products

Comprehensive range of medical imaging equipment

Digital X-ray (DR)

High-resolution digital imaging for immediate diagnostic results. Streamlines workflow with lower radiation dose and superior image quality.

Computerised X-ray (CR)

Cost-effective digital imaging solution using phosphor plates. Bridges the gap between analog and fully digital radiography systems.

X-ray Machine

Reliable and versatile radiographic systems for general diagnostics. Designed for durability and consistent performance in high-volume settings.

C-Arm

Real-time fluoroscopic imaging for surgical and orthopedic procedures. Offers exceptional maneuverability and high-quality visualization.

Pressure Injector

Precision contrast delivery systems for CT and MRI scans. Ensures optimal enhancement and patient safety with automated protocols.

Ultrasound

Advanced sonography systems for obstetrics, cardiology, and general imaging. Delivers crystal-clear images with intuitive user interfaces.

CT

State-of-the-art Computed Tomography scanners for detailed cross-sectional imaging. Fast scan times and high resolution for accurate diagnoses.

MRI

Magnetic Resonance Imaging systems providing exceptional soft tissue contrast. Patient-friendly designs with advanced clinical applications.

X-Ray Films

High-quality medical radiographic films for analog imaging needs. Delivers sharp definition and consistent contrast for reliable reading.
